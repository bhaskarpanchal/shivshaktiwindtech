Shiv Shakti Wind Tech was founded with a primary focus on providing opportunities, support, and growth for our employees and their families. Our commitment extends beyond profit, emphasizing honesty, ethics, and professionalism in all dealings-whether with employees, clients, vendors, or other stakeholders. We prioritize the well-being of our people, recognizing that by genuinely caring for our workforce, clients, and vendors, we ensure the sustained success of our company. Upholding these principles is non-negotiable, and I assert that any deviation where profit outweighs the value of our people warrants the dissolution of the company. Our enduring success is predicated on maintaining this unwavering commitment and ensuring that, guided by ethical principles, we contribute positively to the lives of our employees, clients, and partners. - Laxman Singh Solanki, Founder
Our ISO9001, EN9100, ISO14001, and ISO45001 certifications show our commitment to achieve and maintain solid performance in quality management and environmental impact of our processes, while ensuring the safety and health of our employees at work.
